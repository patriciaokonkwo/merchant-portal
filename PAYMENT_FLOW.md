# Payment Flow Documentation

This document outlines the payment flow process for merchants using the NovaPay platform. It includes steps for initiating payments, handling transactions, and resolving issues related to payment processing.

## Steps in Payment Flow
1. **Initiate Payment**: Merchants can initiate a payment through the dashboard.
2. **Payment Processing**: The system processes the payment and updates the status.
3. **Confirmation**: Merchants receive a confirmation once the payment is successful.
4. **Error Handling**: In case of issues, merchants can refer to the troubleshooting section.

For further details, refer to the specific sections in the portal.
